The net

Instruction

Click the mouse to start both songs and playing

Explanation

"Net" is intended to eliminate worry, tension, and nervousness-related thoughts. By clicking and forming the shapes, you remove your complicated ideas and give your brain a break. It also tries to remind you of the process that occurs in your brain when you are anxious or stressed by depicting the connected shapes and generating the net.
